Discography

Below is a selection of our favourite videos and songs!

Daydream Believer

Written in 1967 by John Stewart, the single reached No. 1 on the US 100 Chart in December of the same year!

Last Train to Clarksville

Our debut single! Released in 1966, this song was played the most out of all our songs on the TV series! Lead vocals for us on this one were performed by Micky Dolenz. This song has been covered by over 10 other bands since its release spanning over 40 years!

(I'm Not Your) Steppin' Stone

Originally written by Tommy Boyce & Bobby Hart, when we played it it was our first B-Side chart record. One of our favourite songs to perform live as it always gets the crowd going! Micky also sang this song as our lead, and during recording we had help from some exceptional people!

I'm A Believer

Last but not least and originally composed by Neil Diamond, our A-side from the same 7" vinyl as Steppin Stone, it was No. 1 in the UK for 4 weeks starting in January 1967. Without a doubt our most recognizable song.
